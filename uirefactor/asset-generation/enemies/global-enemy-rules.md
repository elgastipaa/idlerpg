# Global enemy image rules

Use these rules for every Forge Light enemy asset request.

Generate a single dark fantasy mobile RPG enemy only. The enemy must be centered, full body, readable at mobile combat size, and visually compatible with the current enemy assets from `uirefactor/imagenes.md`. Keep premium painterly-realistic game art, strong silhouette, high contrast, controlled glow, rim light, dark fantasy Forge Light atmosphere, and clean material separation.

Export as a PNG with real alpha transparency. The background must be actually transparent pixels around the enemy. Do not draw a checkerboard, white, gray, black, paper, canvas, cave, arena, floor, glow plate, square card, frame, border, or large cast shadow behind the enemy.

Keep the entire creature inside the canvas with transparent padding around horns, weapons, wings, tail, cloak, feet, and floating parts. Avoid cropped body, partial body, only head, tiny distant figure, background scene, UI, text, letters, numbers, logo, watermark, modern clothing, sci-fi gun, cartoon, chibi, anime, flat vector, blurry silhouette, and low contrast.
