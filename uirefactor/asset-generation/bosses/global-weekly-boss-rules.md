# Global weekly boss image rules

Use these rules for every Forge Light weekly boss asset request.

Generate a single raid-scale weekly boss only. The boss should feel larger, rarer, and more punishing than normal bosses: multi-phase, ceremonial, ancient, and designed for several attempts. Keep a vertical full-body silhouette, centered composition, dominant readable shape, premium painterly-realistic dark fantasy art, high contrast, strong rim light, controlled magical glow, bronze-gold forged accents, and Forge Light atmosphere.

Export as a 1024x1536 PNG with real alpha transparency. The background must be actually transparent pixels around the boss. Do not draw a checkerboard, white, gray, black, paper, canvas, arena, sky, floor, glow plate, card, frame, border, or large cast shadow behind the boss.

Keep the entire boss inside the canvas with extra transparent padding around horns, wings, crown, weapons, tail, cloak, ritual halos, feet, and floating parts. Avoid cropped body, partial body, only head, tiny distant figure, background scene, UI, text, letters, numbers, logo, watermark, modern clothing, sci-fi gun, cartoon, chibi, anime, flat vector, blurry silhouette, and low contrast.

Weekly bosses may include a compact ritual halo, phase crystals, broken chains, or floating relics close to the silhouette, but they must not become a background scene.
