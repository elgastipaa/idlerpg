# Global boss image rules

Use these rules for every Forge Light boss asset request.

Generate a single dark fantasy mobile RPG boss enemy only. The boss must be centered, full body, vertical, and readable in a mobile combat HUD. Keep an imposing boss-scale silhouette, premium painterly-realistic game art, high contrast, strong rim light, controlled glow, bronze-gold forged accents, dark fantasy Forge Light atmosphere, and clear material separation.

Export as a 1024x1536 PNG with real alpha transparency. The background must be actually transparent pixels around the boss. Do not draw a checkerboard, white, gray, black, paper, canvas, cave, arena, sky, floor, glow plate, card, frame, border, or large cast shadow behind the boss. If a preview UI shows a checkerboard, that is acceptable only as transparency preview, but the checkerboard must not be rendered into the image itself.

Keep the entire creature inside the canvas with extra transparent padding around horns, weapons, wings, tail, cloak, feet, and floating parts. Avoid cropped body, partial body, only head, tiny distant figure, background scene, UI, text, letters, numbers, logo, watermark, modern clothing, sci-fi gun, cartoon, chibi, anime, flat vector, blurry silhouette, and low contrast.
