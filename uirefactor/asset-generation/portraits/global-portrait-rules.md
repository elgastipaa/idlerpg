# Global portrait image rules

Use these rules for every Forge Light class portrait.

Generate a single bust portrait only, chest-up or shoulders-up, centered, facing slightly three-quarter view. Keep premium dark fantasy painterly-realistic style, strong readable face/helmet silhouette, high contrast, bronze-gold rim light, controlled class-colored accents, and compatibility with a mobile top bar or hero panel.

Export as a 1024x1024 PNG with real alpha transparency. The background must be actually transparent pixels around the portrait. Do not draw UI, text, logo, watermark, frame, square card, full body, extra characters, scenery, or large cast shadow.

The portrait should still read at small size. Keep armor, hood, weapon hints, or magical effects close to the body.
